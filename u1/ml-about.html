This has been created as a demonstration of interactivity between still renderings. It's purpose is not to showcase a final product but rather to demonstrate intended functionality of buttons and graphics - as we see them working on the app.

 

Be aware, some buttons on this page overlap and the click-able areas in these sections may create confusion.

 

If things aren't working, be patient and try clicking to the left and to the right of your intended target. Or consult the menu at the bottom of the screen for a targeted approach to the screens.

 

If there are any questions, do not hesitate to email karol.buczek@localtopia.us
